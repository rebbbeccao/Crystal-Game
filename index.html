Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random, "Magic Number" at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below, By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the Magic Number. However you loose the game if your score goes above the Magic Number.

Easy right? Here's the catch...

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Click wearily! At the start of each game the crystals value will change.
